About Us

SheCode AASTU is a community dedicated to empowering and supporting women in technology at the Addis Ababa Science and Technology University (AASTU). The group focuses on providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities for women interested in coding and tech-related fields.

How it goes

Step 1: Register for the Program

Begin your journey with SheCode AASTU by registering through our easy-to-use online portal.

Step 2: Personalized Mentee Assignments

Once registered, our team will carefully match you with mentees based on your specific interests and goals

Step 3: Collaborative Group Formation

These groups are designed to foster teamwork, exchange of ideas, and collective problem-solving, creating a dynamic learning environment.

Step 4: Mentor Assignments and Guidance

Each group will be assigned experienced mentors who will provide continuous support, advice, and mentorship.

Step 5: Engaging Online Classes

Participate in our comprehensive online classes, equipped with full resources, including lectures, tutorials, and interactive sessions.

Step 6: Final Project Showcase

This project will be a testament to your hard work and growth, providing you with a portfolio piece to showcase to future employers or collaborators.
